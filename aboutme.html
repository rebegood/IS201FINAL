About Me

Please enjoy this page about my personal life. For additional information about my professional experience, Please see my resume section of my website.

Biography

I was born September 11, 2000 in Singapore. I moved to Salt Lake City when I was 5 and have lived in Utah ever since. I served a full-time mission in Vina Del Mar, Chile. I have three siblings and two french bulldogs who are basically my siblings as well. The four most important aspects of my life can be summarized in the following list:
  1. Jesus
  2. Family
  3. Friends
  4. Fitness

Jesus

I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ and have been my whole life. I have a strong belief in Jesus Christ and I love to serve others as He has taught me to. I served my mission in the Chile, Vina Del Mar mission and it was the most important experience of my life as it allowed me to develop faith in Christ.

 

For more information about the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, see this website. https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/?lang=eng"

 

Family

I am the third of four children and my family is very close. My parents live in Salt Lake so I see them most weekends and my younger brother is studying math at BYU so we get to hang out a lot. My family enjoys traveling and we have been on many international adventures together.

 

 

Friends

When I am not busy with work or school, I am often spending time with my best friends. They are the goofiest clowns ever and we laugh so hard together. I love them because they are so fun and supportive. They are always cheering me on in my goals, and I love to support them in their pursuits.

 

 

fitness

I grew up swimming competitively and have always loved being part of a team. I swam for BYU for two years and specialized in long distance events. When I returned home from my mission, I got into weight lifting and it has become my favorite hobby. I train six days a week and it is a source of peace in my daily life. Fun Fact: I can hip thrust 300 pounds!
